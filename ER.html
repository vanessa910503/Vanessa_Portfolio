TMBA – Equity Research (J&V Energy Technology Co)



Established in 2016 by a finance expert with over a decade of experience, J&V Energy Technology Co., Ltd. combines investor funds with solar power plant construction, offering comprehensive one-stop services to clients. They leverage their expertise in land development, construction, project management, and O&M for Taiwan's solar power plants. By managing costs in-house, they deliver efficient, top-quality services.

My Contribution



  • Studying J&V Energy for investment suitability and selecting the investment method (conclusion: Buy the stock, and it indeed saw significant subsequent growth)
  • Mainly researched the solar energy industry overview and the future growth potential of J&V Energy. Focused on how government green energy policies would impact the company and potential projects
  • Generated professional investment reports and presented the findings to the directors

Equity Research Knowledge
