IBM – GreenEloan with AI & ESG Integration



This project was undertaken during my summer internship at IBM in 2023. Its primary objective was to assist IBM in creating a tool that integrates ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) with generative AI to streamline the credit assessment process within the banking industry.

My Contribution



  • Scraping relevant publicly available ESG data from the internet
  • Utilizing OpenAI's Langchain to integrate data, which can read various file formats such as JSON, PDF, CSV, and simultaneously feed different file types into the model.
  • Using OpenAI's ChatGPT to analyze the scraped data and respond to credit assessment-related questions.
  • Researching and testing Prompt Engineering techniques, including CoT (Conversational AI for Tasks), Few-Shot Learning, and synonym usage optimization.
