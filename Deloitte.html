Deloitte Consulting Project – Insurance / Reinsurance Annual Report



This was a project I worked on during my internship at Deloitte. Its primary goal was to help my team gain a better understanding of the overall framework used by foreign reinsurance and insurance companies when creating annual reports. The focus was primarily on disclosure methods related to ESG, sustainability, company structure, and more. The intention was to assist colleagues in various initiatives, such as digital transformation projects and client acquisition, within the company.

My Contribution



  • Designed the overall presentation structure and handled graphic design
  • Focused on researching business reports and sustainability reports for Swiss reinsurance companies
  • Conducted research on company governance and sustainability for Zurich insurance companies
  • The research I conducted on TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures) paved the way for my involvement in projects during my internship at IBM

Sustainability Knowledge

Consulting Skills
