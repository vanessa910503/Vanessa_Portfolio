Travel Assistant – Predicting Tourist Footfall and Hotel Room Prices



This is a project I worked on at school, primarily aimed at using machine learning to address various challenges people face during their travels. The ultimate model can accurately predict future Airbnb prices and tourist attraction foot traffic for the upcoming days. This not only assists travelers in making better choices regarding travel destinations and accommodations but also helps travel industry professionals adjust pricing and operational strategies in a timely manner.

My Contribution



  • Identifying insights from the data and making corresponding decisions and recommendations.
  • Data collection and creation of relevant charts
  • Design and development of the final presentation website
  • Utilizing K-means and DNN (Deep Neural Network) models for predicting tourist attraction foot traffic and Airbnb room prices.




Machine Learning

  • Through this project, I've gained the knowledge of fine-tuning basic models to enhance their predictive capabilities, which has been a valuable skill in making accurate forecasts.

Data Analysis

  • Through this project, I have learned how to organize extensive datasets, extract valuable information, conduct in-depth analysis, and formulate corresponding strategies.

Teamwork

  • Throughout this project, I developed effective communication and collaboration skills with individuals from diverse backgrounds.
