This is a game I developed using the Python library, Pygame. The inspiration behind it comes from a close friend of mine, Angela, who smokes despite my earnest advice against it. So, I decided to create this game as a reminder of the dangers of smoking and encourage her to quit. However, it did not work.

  • The game consists of three levels, representing the number of cigarettes she smokes in a week (actual gameplay difficulty is determined by the speed of moving obstacles)
  • The score is calculated based on her expected lifespan
  • Each time she plays, if she collides with an obstacle, she will have to restart
  • The target score for each level is 57 (the same as my friend's academic achievement score). Once the score reaches 57, a video about cancer cell mutation will be shown as a warning

Video Game Design

Logic Training

  • Designing different levels and events that trigger various actions for the player has significantly enhanced my logical thinking abilities.
